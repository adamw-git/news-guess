Rules

Here is how to play the game:

Goal:
Guess where the story takes place in the shortest amount of time with the least amount of mistakes

You start with 1000 points and three guesses. Each mistake is -200 points

Each hint reveals information about the article:
The 1st hint reveals the date the article was published.

The 2nd and last hint reveals the image.

You will also be timed! Each second, you lose 5 points. So the quicker you guess, the higher your score will be!

Have fun!
