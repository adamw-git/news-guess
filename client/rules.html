Rules

Here is how to play the game:

Goal:
Guess the location of the article in the shortest amount of time with the least amount of hints.

You start with 1000 points and three hints.

Each hint reveals information about the article, but at the cost of your score:

The 1st hint reveals the publication date at the cost of 50 points
The 2nd hint reveals the name at the cost of 100 points
The 3rd hint reveals the image at the cost of 200 points

You will also be timed, so the longer you take to guess, the lower your score will be!

Have fun!
